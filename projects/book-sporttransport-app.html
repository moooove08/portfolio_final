Book Sport Transport

Commercial-style iOS booking prototype

A polished booking-style iOS prototype for sport transport services: catalogue browsing, customer details, pickup address, date/time selection, confirmation and map-style booking state.

Product flow

What the app demonstrates

  • Transport/service catalogue with bike and scooter-style categories
  • Customer booking form with name, phone, pickup address and date/time slots
  • Booking confirmation state with selected transport and scheduled time
  • Map-style pickup/status screen for a customer-facing service experience
  • Client-pass-ready flow that can be extended with QR confirmation or digital access card

Technical focus

Implementation highlights

  • Structured a complete customer journey from discovery to booking confirmation
  • Designed production-style UI states around booking, customer details, route confirmation and client pass scenarios
  • Separated the prototype narrative into catalogue, booking and confirmation modules
  • Structured the experience the way a real product could use REST APIs, MapKit-based route context and QR-enabled confirmation flows

Portfolio note

Source code is not public. The case study focuses on a commercial-style mobile flow centered on UIKit screens, customer journey, booking UX and production-style presentation. The product direction is well suited for REST APIs, Firebase-backed customer state, MapKit route handling and QR-based booking confirmation.
