Chickens & Beast

Local multiplayer party-game prototype

A local multiplayer party-game prototype where players receive hidden roles, move through daily events, discuss suspicious behavior, vote and try to survive while the Beast hunts the group.

Product flow

What the app demonstrates

  • Local multiplayer flow for 5 to 9 players
  • Hidden role assignment with custom role cards
  • Player name entry and duplicate-name validation
  • Event-driven day progression, discussion and voting
  • Player elimination and win/lose end screens

Technical focus

Implementation highlights

  • Organized gameplay responsibility across main game, player selection, discussion and turn flow files
  • Modeled roles, events, durations and player state
  • Built a complete playable prototype with custom screens and assets
  • Good example of complex screen flow beyond standard list/forms

Portfolio note

Public GitHub repository is available. The README documents the local multiplayer game loop, UIKit/XIB structure, role cards, events, timed discussion and voting flow.
